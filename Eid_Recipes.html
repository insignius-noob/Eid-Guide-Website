Sheer Khurma

Sheer Khurma is a traditional vermicelli pudding made with milk, sugar, nuts, and dates. It is often prepared during Eid celebrations in many Muslim households.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 liter full cream milk
  2. 100 grams vermicelli (seviyan)
  3. 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  4. 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  5. 1/4 cup assorted dry fruits (almonds, pistachios, cashews)
  6. 1/4 cup dates, chopped
  7. 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  8. Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and roast vermicelli until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add milk and bring it to a boil. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Add sugar, roasted vermicelli, and cardamom powder. Cook until vermicelli is soft.
  4. Add chopped dates and assorted dry fruits. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with saffron strands and serve hot or chilled.

Meat Karahi

Meat Karahi is a popular and flavorful dish, especially during Eid ul Adha. This spicy and aromatic dish is cooked with tender meat, tomatoes, and a blend of spices.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 kg mutton or beef, cut into pieces
  2. 4 large tomatoes, chopped
  3. 1/2 cup oil or ghee
  4. 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  5. 1 tsp red chili powder
  6. 1 tsp cumin powder
  7. 1 tsp coriander powder
  8. Salt to taste
  9. Fresh green chilies, for garnish
  10. Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish
  11. 1 tsp black pepper, freshly ground
  12. 1 tsp garam masala

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and add the ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add the meat and cook on high heat until it changes color.
  3. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil starts to separate.
  4. Lower the heat, cover the pan, and cook the meat until tender (you may add a little water if necessary).
  5. Once the meat is cooked and the oil separates, add freshly ground black pepper and garam masala. Stir well.
  6. Garnish with fresh green chilies and coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan or roti.
